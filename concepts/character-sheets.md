## Sections

### 1. **Header**
- **Avatar** â€” large pixel art sprite (128x128 or 64x64)

- **Energy** â€” current stamina (low energy = needs rest)
- **Quote** â€” personality flavor text

### 2. **Stats**

- **Tasks Completed** â€” total quests finished
- **Success Rate** â€” % of tasks completed without errors
- **Avg Time** â€” mean time per task
- **Lines of Code** â€” total lines written/modified
- **Tests Written** â€” count of test cases added
- **Bugs Fixed** â€” count of fixes
- **Files Modified** â€” total files touched
